Carbon Efficiency Rating is a web application for furniture manufacturing companies to efficiently calculate and generate PDF certificates which score the carbon footprint of their products. The app helps to empower businesses to track their environmental impact and drive positive change within their industry.

Commissioned by a startup company, this project involved the creation of a number of key high-fidelity screens and a website landing page.
